Explanation

This chart shows the monthly sales volume for this year from January 2020 until June 2020 by customer group.

Insights

1 General sales volumes are dropping since February 2020.

2 Biggest drop in Businesses (B2B), where sales have dropped over 76% since January.

3 Sales for all customer groups but Government (Public) have dropped between 15% - 25% on a monthly basis since February.

4 Either a global or organisation-level event seems to be affecting sales as all groups but one seem to be affected in similar manner.

5 Government (Public) group sales have been increased 240% since January.
